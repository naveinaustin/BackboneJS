This project shows creating and advanced search panel with auto complete.

A single line search with prompts. You can search by name, emailid, term words. Operators are LIKE or =. Join operators are AND and OR. Focus in the input field and press spacebar. It will suggest you words.
After each selection continue pressing spacebar.
Suggestions will not appear after LIKE and = as you have to enter values. After you have entered values you can press spacebar to continue.
